Brand Overview

This comprehensive style guide defines the visual identity of Altered Security, ensuring consistency across all digital platforms. The brand identity reflects our focus on advanced cybersecurity training with a professional, modern, and trustworthy aesthetic.

Brand Personality

  • Professional - Authoritative and trustworthy
  • Technical - Expert and precise
  • Modern - Contemporary and innovative
  • Bold - Confident and impactful

Brand Voice

  • Authoritative - Expert but accessible
  • Clear - Direct and jargon-free when possible
  • Confident - Assured without being arrogant
  • Educational - Informative and helpful

Color Usage Guidelines

Do's

  • Use Altered Red for primary CTAs and important highlights
  • Maintain sufficient contrast for text readability
  • Use gradients sparingly for visual interest

Don'ts

  • Avoid using too many accent colors in one section
  • Don't place red text on dark backgrounds
  • Avoid using colors not in the official palette

Typography Guidelines

Do's

  • Use Montserrat for headings and Inter for body text
  • Maintain proper hierarchy with consistent heading levels
  • Use JetBrains Mono for code and technical content

Don'ts

  • Don't mix too many font weights in one section
  • Avoid using fonts not specified in this guide
  • Don't use text smaller than 14px for body content

Content Hierarchy

Our content follows a clear hierarchy to guide users through the page:

  1. 1

    Primary Heading

    Clear, descriptive page title that communicates the main purpose

  2. 2

    Supporting Information

    Brief description or context that supports the main heading

  3. 3

    Primary Call to Action

    The main action we want users to take

  4. 4

    Secondary Content

    Supporting content organized in sections with clear headings

  5. 5

    Secondary CTAs

    Alternative actions or next steps for users

Layout Principles

Key principles that guide our layout decisions:

  • Consistency

    Maintain consistent spacing, alignment, and component usage across all pages

  • White Space

    Use generous white space to improve readability and focus attention

  • Responsive Design

    All layouts adapt gracefully to different screen sizes and devices

  • F-Pattern Reading

    Organize content to follow natural reading patterns (F-pattern for text-heavy pages)

  • Visual Balance

    Create visual balance through symmetry or intentional asymmetry

Accessibility & Usability

Our platform is designed to be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities, and to provide an intuitive, user-friendly experience.

Color Contrast

We maintain sufficient color contrast to ensure readability for all users, including those with visual impairments.

Text on Light Backgrounds

Dark text on white (Ratio: 12:1) ✓

Red accent on white (Ratio: 4.5:1) ✓

Gray text on white (Ratio: 4.5:1) ✓

Text on Dark Backgrounds

White text on dark (Ratio: 12:1) ✓

Red accent on dark (Ratio: 3:1) ✓

Light gray on dark (Ratio: 4:1) ✓

Contrast Guidelines:

  • Maintain at least 4.5:1 contrast for normal text
  • Maintain at least 3:1 contrast for large text
  • Don't rely on color alone to convey information
